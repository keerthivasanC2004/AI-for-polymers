# Polymer Data Registry, 2025-2026

This registry separates the audited polymer resources into three operational collections.

## Collections

- **Large-scale / more data:** at least 10,000 records/configurations/spectra, multi-property coverage at roughly 25+ properties, or multi-gigabyte trajectory archives.
- **Highly curated / modeling-ready:** paper- or institution-linked, structured, provenance-rich, and suitable for reproducible modeling.
- **External and review:** specialized, embargoed, low-structure, or provenance-sensitive resources.

A resource may appear in both the large-scale and highly curated lists. Non-GitHub sources cannot be forked; they are tracked by persistent URL/DOI and should be accessed through downloader scripts or metadata manifests.
